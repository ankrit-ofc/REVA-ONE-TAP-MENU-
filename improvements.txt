C. (Answer) Card & Wallet are NOT linked to a gateway
Traced in payment_service.py:
Counter CASH / CARD / COUNTER_WALLET → record_counter_payment just marks the invoice DRAFT → PAID, records the method, closes the order. No gateway call, no verification — it's a manual "staff collected it at the counter" record (CARD = an external card machine; COUNTER_WALLET = eSewa/Khalti taken manually).
Only QR_GATEWAY (the customer paying online from their phone) is integrated: create_payment_intent → gateway → webhook (handle_webhook) verifies signature + amount.

So your read is correct: card/wallet at the counter are trust-based records, not automated charges. Recommendation: keep them manual (normal for counter ops).
